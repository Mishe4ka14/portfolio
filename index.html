Stellar-burger

Classic student work. With the interactive constructor, simply grab and drag ingredients to create the burger of your dream. And your personal account guarantees that not a single alien will know the order number except you.

Pet-project Hedwig

Pet-project of a simple messenger. Here you can find a friend and chat with him (the only condition is to tell him about Hedwig and ask to register). Then websocket technology will download your messages even without reloading the page. When registering, you will have an avatar of a cute elf, but at any time you can change it in your profile. In general, it is almost like Telegram, only a little simpler =).
The project is actively being developed and new features are planned to be added.

Kupi-podari

Wishlist service. After registration, you can create your own gift selections. Do you want a new gadget, fashionable clothes, or just a book? Easily! Add any gifts to the list, indicating their price and the store where you can buy them. This will help your friends understand what exactly you need and where you can buy it. Your friends can view your wishlist and choose which ones to donate to.
The project is uploaded to the server using Docker.
